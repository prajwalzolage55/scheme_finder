About SchemeSaathi

A free, AI-powered platform to help every Indian family discover and access government welfare schemes they're entitled to.

🎯 Our Mission

Every year, crores of rupees in government welfare benefits go unclaimed because eligible families simply don't know about them or don't know how to apply. SchemeSaathi bridges this gap by making scheme discovery instant, intelligent, and accessible to everyone — in any language, on any device.

📖 How to Use SchemeSaathi

  • 1
    Create a Free Account

    Register with your name, email, and phone number. Completely free — no charges ever.

  • 2
    Fill the Eligibility Form

    Enter income, age, gender, caste, location, and occupation. Takes about 60 seconds.

  • 3
    View Your Matched Schemes

    See all schemes you're eligible for with a match score, benefits, and documents needed.

  • 4
    Follow the Apply Guide

    Step-by-step instructions tell you exactly where to go and what to do to apply.

  • 5
    Use the AI Chatbot for Doubts

    Ask anything in Hindi, Marathi, or English. Get instant, personalised answers.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

SchemeSaathi provides informational guidance only. Eligibility shown is based on the information you provide and may not be 100% accurate. Always verify eligibility at the official government portal or nearest government office. We are not affiliated with the Government of India.
